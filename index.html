About

Most small businesses entering the U.S. Federal market face the same challenges: compliance feels overwhelming, proposals miss key details, and opportunities slip by because targeting and timing aren’t precise. That’s where I come in.

With 15+ years of experience in mechanical engineering, heavy industry, and military operations, I’ve built a career around making complex systems work under pressure. I now apply that discipline to federal contracting—helping small businesses close the gaps that keep them from winning.

  • If your proposals aren’t compliant — I bring structure and story so they pass review.
  • If your capture process lacks focus — I design a system to target the right agencies at the right time.
  • If your internal processes are ad-hoc — I turn them into repeatable, auditable workflows that withstand scrutiny.

I don’t just advise—I improve the systems you already have, turning uncertainty into repeatable success.

How I Work

  • Pinpoint where your capture and proposal systems break down.
  • Build lean, compliant processes that align with federal requirements.
  • Target mechanical, technology, and security opportunities with precision.
  • Deliver proposals and pipelines that can withstand scrutiny from agencies like DoD, Homeland Security, Justice, and VA.

When small businesses fall short, I step in to turn weak spots into winning strategies—so you don’t just compete, you succeed.
