About

Most equipment and technical firms face similar challenges when approaching federal contracts—complex compliance, unclear requirements, and tight schedules. That’s where we come in.

With over 15 years in mechanical engineering, maintenance, and defense operations, I understand both the technical and procedural sides of contracting. Bonny Contracting Solutions helps companies bridge that gap—turning capability into contract performance.

  • If your proposals miss the mark — I bring clarity and structure so your technical strengths translate into compliant, persuasive bids.
  • If your capture process lacks focus — I map the right DoD and State opportunities that fit your production and service capacity.
  • If your internal systems are ad-hoc — I turn them into repeatable workflows that withstand review and support future bids.

I align operations, compliance, and capture to help industrial firms scale sustainably into the federal market.

How I Work

  • Identify where your capture and proposal systems break down.
  • Build compliant workflows that integrate with your existing maintenance or technical systems.
  • Target DoD, VA, and Homeland Security opportunities tied to equipment, leasing, or service support.
  • Develop proposals and reporting processes that withstand audits and performance reviews.

When operational teams fall short, I help convert reliability into federal-winning strategies—so you don’t just compete, you deliver.
