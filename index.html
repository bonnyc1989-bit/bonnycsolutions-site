About

I deliver results where risk, regulation, and timelines are tight.
Across 15 years in the British Army’s Royal Engineers and heavy industry—offshore rigs, rail rolling stock, mining, and advanced manufacturing—I learned to plan precisely, execute safely, and keep complex systems running under pressure. That field experience now powers my work helping small businesses find, pursue, and win U.S. Federal contracts.

In uniform, I was trained in demolitions and IED clearance, and I led engineering tasks in bridging and mobility support—where discipline, safety, and logistics decide outcomes. In industry, I commissioned rolling stock, maintained heavy plant and offshore equipment, and supervised multi‑shift crews on major capital programs. The common thread: complex requirements, unforgiving schedules, and compliance that cannot be an afterthought.

I translate that mindset into federal business development: targeting the right departments, shaping early with credible technical conversations, and building proposals that are compliant, defensible, and easy to evaluate. I don’t chase noise; I build a pipeline you can win and a plan you can deliver. My focus includes the Departments of Defense, State, Homeland Security, Justice, and Veterans Affairs.

How I work

  • Clear target list and outreach rhythm aligned to your strengths.
  • Bid/No‑Bid discipline with structured capture actions and review gates.
  • Proposals that are compliant on day one—traceable, lean, and story‑driven.
  • Delivery credibility baked in: staffing, risk, schedule, and QA that withstand scrutiny.

If you want growth without wasted effort, I bring calm, structure, and momentum to every opportunity.
