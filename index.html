Services

Practical, outcome-focused consulting for manufacturers, equipment lessors, and maintenance firms seeking to enter or expand within the U.S. Federal market. Each engagement is built around your capacity, compliance needs, and competitive position.

Strategic Positioning & Federal Readiness

Build the foundation to win: align your machinery, maintenance, or fabrication capabilities with federal demand. I assess your readiness across compliance, registrations, and certifications and design a clear roadmap for entry.

Pipeline Development & Opportunity Shaping

Identify high-value contracts before they hit SAM.gov. Using data from DoD, VA, and State Department sources, I help you target and influence upcoming solicitations that match your production, leasing, and service capacity—so you bid smarter, not harder.

Capture & Proposal Support for Industrial Contracts

Translate your technical experience into compliant, compelling proposals. From Statement of Work alignment to past performance narratives, I strengthen submissions for equipment supply, heavy maintenance, and mechanical service contracts.

Lifecycle Bidding Systems & Ongoing Support

Once you’re in the system, stay competitive. I help you build repeatable capture processes and compliance templates so your company can pursue multiple federal opportunities efficiently—without reinventing the wheel each time.

About

Most equipment and manufacturing companies face the same problem breaking into federal contracting: compliance feels overwhelming, proposal structures are unclear, and timing rarely aligns with production cycles. That’s where I come in.

With over 15 years in mechanical engineering, heavy machinery maintenance, and industrial operations, I understand both the shop floor and the contracting process. Now, I help businesses in manufacturing, machinery leasing, and equipment maintenance build federal strategies that turn technical expertise into long-term contracts.

  • If your proposals miss the mark — I bring clarity and structure so your technical strengths translate into compliant, persuasive bids.
  • If your business lacks capture focus — I map the right DoD and State opportunities that match your production and service capacity.
  • If your internal systems are ad-hoc — I turn them into repeatable, auditable workflows that withstand government review.

I don’t just advise; I align operations, compliance, and capture to help industrial firms scale into federal markets—sustainably.

  • Identify where your bid, capture, and compliance processes break down.
  • Build lean, compliant workflows that integrate with your existing production and maintenance systems.
  • Target DoD, VA, and Homeland Security opportunities that require equipment supply, leasing, or maintenance.
  • Develop proposals and reporting systems that can withstand technical and compliance audits.

When industrial companies fall short, I step in to convert operational reliability into federal-winning strategies—so you don’t just compete, you deliver.
