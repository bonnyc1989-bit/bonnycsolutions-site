About

I’ve spent my career where operations meet outcomes — managing suppliers, leading bids, and delivering commercial commitments in high-pressure environments. Before consulting, I served as a Royal Engineer. That experience shaped my approach: clear communication, disciplined execution, and a bias for results.

Today I bring that practical mindset to federal contracting. I translate government requirements into simple, actionable plans: target the right agencies, shape the opportunity early, and submit proposals that are compliant and compelling. When the engagement requires more hands, we execute as a team through BonnyCsolutions.

If you’re serious about growth but tired of wasted effort, we’ll be a good fit. I’ll meet you where you are, align the strategy to your capabilities, and push each opportunity forward with a calm, disciplined process.
