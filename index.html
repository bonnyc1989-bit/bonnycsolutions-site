About

I bring 15 years of combined military and commercial operations together in engineering. Using these skills I’ve gained, I now help small businesses find, pursue, and win federal contracts—turning requirements into clear, actionable work.

My role is to bridge your capabilities with the public sector: targeting the right agencies, shaping early, and submitting proposals that are compliant and compelling. When a project needs more hands, I bring in trusted specialists to fulfill the requirements put forward by the federal government.

If you want growth without wasted effort, we’ll be a good fit. I meet you where you are, align strategy to your strengths, and push each opportunity forward with a calm, disciplined process.
